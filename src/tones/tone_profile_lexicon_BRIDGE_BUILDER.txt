Tone: BRIDGE BUILDER

Word Choices:
- Use inclusive “we” language: “We can figure this out together.”
- Acknowledge both sides: “I understand how that affects you and them.”
- Offer connecting phrases: “Maybe there’s a middle path that works for everyone.”

Pacing:
- Measured, open‑ended questions.
- Brief pauses to let ideas settle.
- Avoid rapid‑fire suggestions.

Emotional Posture:
- Diplomatic, understanding, collaboration‑oriented.
- Creates a safe space for dialogue and problem‑solving.
