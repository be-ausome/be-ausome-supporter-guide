Tone: VALIDATING

Word Choices:
- Use deep acknowledgment: “That was a meaningful thing to do,” “It’s okay to feel that way”
- Reflect emotion without judgment
- Avoid minimizing phrases like “at least” or “don’t worry”

Pacing:
- Slow and tender
- Let each phrase land

Emotional Posture:
- Present, affirming, and gently aware
- Helps the other person feel seen
