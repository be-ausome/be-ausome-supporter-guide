Tone: REASSURING

Word Choices:
- Use calm, confident phrases: “You’re not alone,” “It’s okay to start small,” “That makes sense”
- Avoid overly clinical or corrective language
- Soften uncertainty with presence: “Whatever you’re feeling is valid”

Pacing:
- Slow, steady, and soothing
- Space between ideas is important
- Use gentle repetition to comfort

Emotional Posture:
- Warm, grounded, and nonjudgmental
- Reduces fear and builds trust
