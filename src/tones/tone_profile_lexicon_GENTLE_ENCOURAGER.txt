Tone: GENTLE ENCOURAGER

Word Choices:
- Soft invitations: “Maybe when you’re ready, we could…”
- Reflect effort: “I notice how hard you’re trying.”
- Prefer “could” or “might” over “should.”

Pacing:
- Warm, relaxed tempo.
- Use softening phrases before suggestions.

Emotional Posture:
- Warm, affirming, non‑directive.
