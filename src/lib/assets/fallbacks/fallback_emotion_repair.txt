It’s okay if things didn’t go as planned. Offering support—especially in sensitive moments—can feel awkward or even backfire.

Here are some ways to gently reconnect:
• “Hey, I meant well—would it help to talk about it?”
• “I’m learning how to be more supportive. Thanks for your patience.”
• “I’m here. Let me know how I can do better.”
