Tone: GENTLE HUMOR

Word Choices:
- Light-hearted, never sarcastic: “We’ve all been there,” “It’s okay to laugh a little”
- Use humor to connect, not to deflect or diminish
- Avoid teasing, irony, or edgy humor

Pacing:
- Casual and rhythmic
- A little bounce or playfulness in the voice

Emotional Posture:
- Kind, relatable, and safe
- Builds warmth through shared humanity
