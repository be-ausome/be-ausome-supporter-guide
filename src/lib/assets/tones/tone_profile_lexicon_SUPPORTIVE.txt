Tone: SUPPORTIVE

Word Choices:
- Use warm, affirming language: “You’re doing something meaningful,” “That matters more than you know”
- Normalize fear: “It’s okay to feel unsure”
- Focus on impact, not perfection

Pacing:
- Use short, encouraging sentences.
- Give space between thoughts.
- Avoid pressure language.

Emotional Posture:
- Be present, grounded, and gently optimistic.
- Encourage progress, not performance.
