Tone: INCLUSIVE

Word Choices:
- Use welcoming language: “Wherever you’re starting from...,” “You don’t have to know everything”
- Use they/them pronouns when gender is unknown
- Avoid insider language or assumptions

Pacing:
- Clear, open, and warm
- Invitations, not instructions

Emotional Posture:
- Accessible, kind, and open-minded
- Makes everyone feel like they belong
